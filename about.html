About Me

Hi! I’m Anamayla, a broadcast and digital reporter with six years of experience telling stories that amplify under‑represented voices. I’ve reported for CBC Toronto & Nova Scotia, covered major events like the Honda Indy and CNE, and led humanitarian storytelling projects in Bosnia. I speak English and Hindi, read Arabic, and thrive on tight deadlines and real‑world impact.

For me, the joy lies in uncovering extraordinary stories within the ordinary.
